Lotus

Lotus is the national flower of both India and Vietnam. Interestingly, it is the Lotus pink flowers that are a national symbol. It is an aquatic flower with round leaves floating on the water. It grows in freshwater lakes and shallow ponds.

It is a beautiful flower which has an appealing fragrance and colour. The flower is usually white or light pink in colour with boat-shaped petals. The leaves of a lotus flower are large and dark green in colour. The stem of the flower is very thick and it has a glowing yellow centre. It is considered sacred and is often used for religious purposes.The lotus flower is also used for decoration purposes in temples and marriage functions.
